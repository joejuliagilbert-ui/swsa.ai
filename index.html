FAQ

Do you install in Albuquerque and Santa Fe?

Yes. If you are in Albuquerque or Santa Fe, call or text and I will confirm availability and scheduling.

Do you install in Farmington and Durango?

Yes. Farmington is my home base, and I also take installs in Durango and the Four Corners region.

What is the best starter system?

For most homes, the best start is basic entry protection (doors/windows) plus a doorbell camera or a front-entry outdoor camera. From there, you expand based on your layout and what you actually want to monitor.

Can I add cameras, sensors, or smart locks later?

Yes. Many customers start with core protection and expand later once they live with the system and understand what they want next.

Do cameras require monitoring?

Not always. Monitoring and cameras solve different problems. I can help you decide what makes sense for your goals and budget.

Will the system work if Wi-Fi goes out?

Your setup depends on the equipment in the home. When we plan the install, I explain what uses Wi-Fi, what does not, and what backup options exist.

How do I get a quote?

Call or text me with your city and what you want (security, cameras, smart lock, or a mix). I’ll ask a few quick questions and give you a clear next step.
