What a Practical ADT Setup Includes

Most homes start with entry protection and expand based on layout and goals. A properly configured system typically includes:

  • Door and window sensors for entry protection
  • Motion detection in key interior areas (placed to avoid nuisance alerts)
  • Glass break detection where large windows or sliding doors create risk
  • Smart locks for controlled entry and user management (when desired)
  • Camera options based on sightlines and real entry points
